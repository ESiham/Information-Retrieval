<p><i><b>Rain Shadow</b></i> is an <a href="/wiki/Australia" title="Australia">Australian</a> <a href="/wiki/Television" title="Television">television</a> <a href="/wiki/Drama" title="Drama">drama</a> series which premiered on 7 October 2007 on <a href="/wiki/ABC_Television" title="ABC Television">ABC TV</a>. It aired on Sundays at <span class="nowrap">8.30 pm</span>. The six-part series (6 X 1 hour) was produced by <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Southern_Star_Group" title="Southern Star Group">Southern Star</a>. Music from <a href="/wiki/The_Audreys" title="The Audreys">The Audreys</a> features in the soundtrack for the show, including the main theme.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p><i>Rain Shadow</i> was shot in the <a href="/wiki/Adelaide_Hills" title="Adelaide Hills">Adelaide Hills</a> in <a href="/wiki/South_Australia" title="South Australia">South Australia</a> and is set in the fictional district of Paringa, a dry land farming area in a <a href="/wiki/Rain_shadow" title="Rain shadow">rain shadow</a>. It tells the story of two characters who become the means of each other's future. It stars <a href="/wiki/Rachel_Ward" title="Rachel Ward">Rachel Ward</a> as district vet Kate McDonald and <a href="/wiki/Victoria_Thaine" title="Victoria Thaine">Victoria Thaine</a> as new veterinary assistant Jill Blake.</p>
