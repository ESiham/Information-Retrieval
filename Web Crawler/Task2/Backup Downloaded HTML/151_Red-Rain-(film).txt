<p><i><b>Red Rain</b></i> is a 2013 <a href="/wiki/Science_fiction" title="Science fiction">science fiction</a> <a href="/wiki/Thriller_film" title="Thriller film">thriller film</a> written and directed by Rahul Sadasivan. The film stars <a href="/wiki/Narain_(actor)" title="Narain (actor)">Narain</a>, <a href="/wiki/Mohan_Sharma" title="Mohan Sharma">Mohan Sharma</a>, <a href="/wiki/Tini_Tom" title="Tini Tom">Tini Tom</a>, <a href="/wiki/Shari_(actress)" title="Shari (actress)">Shari</a>, <a href="/wiki/Devan_(actor)" title="Devan (actor)">Devan</a>, <a href="/wiki/Leona_Lishoy" title="Leona Lishoy">Leona Lishoy</a>, Andrea Fortis and Sergio Kalei. <i>Red Rain</i> was produced by Sachin Sadasivan under the banner <i>Highlands Entertainment</i> and released theatrically in India on 6 December 2013. The story focuses on a young researcher named Jay (Narain) who becomes convinced that the strange phenomena are a result of <a href="/wiki/Extraterrestrial_life" title="Extraterrestrial life">extraterrestrial life</a>. The film was loosely based on the <a href="/wiki/Red_rain_in_Kerala" title="Red rain in Kerala">Kerala red rain phenomenon</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>

<p><i>Red rain</i> received generally positive feedback from audience. The subject seems elusive and deep for the normal class but it sure had a fan following from an A class audience. After its <a href="/wiki/Premiere" title="Premiere">premiere</a> on television, many praised on Rahul’s Direction and Narain’s performance.
