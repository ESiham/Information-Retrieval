<p>"<b>Rain Dance</b>" is a song written by <a href="/wiki/Burton_Cummings" title="Burton Cummings">Burton Cummings</a> and <a href="/wiki/Kurt_Winter" title="Kurt Winter">Kurt Winter</a> and performed by <a href="/wiki/The_Guess_Who" title="The Guess Who">The Guess Who</a>. It reached #3 in <a href="/wiki/RPM_(magazine)" title="RPM (magazine)">Canada</a>, #8 in New Zealand,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> #19 on the <a href="/wiki/Billboard_Hot_100" title="Billboard Hot 100"><i>Billboard</i> Hot 100</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> and #55 in <a href="/wiki/Kent_Music_Report" title="Kent Music Report">Australia</a> in 1971. It was featured on their 1971 album, <i><a href="/wiki/So_Long,_Bannatyne" title="So Long, Bannatyne">So Long, Bannatyne</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
<p>The song was produced by <a href="/wiki/Jack_Richardson_(record_producer)" title="Jack Richardson (record producer)">Jack Richardson</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
