<p><i><b>Rain Dances</b></i> is the fifth studio album by English <a href="/wiki/Progressive_rock" title="Progressive rock">progressive rock</a> band <a href="/wiki/Camel_(band)" title="Camel (band)">Camel</a>. It was released in 1977 on Gama Records/<a href="/wiki/Decca_Records" title="Decca Records">Decca Records</a>, and brought a major change to the band's lineup, by replacing bassist Doug Ferguson with ex-<a href="/wiki/Caravan_(band)" title="Caravan (band)">Caravan</a> member <a href="/wiki/Richard_Sinclair" title="Richard Sinclair">Richard Sinclair</a> and by adding saxophonist <a href="/wiki/Mel_Collins" title="Mel Collins">Mel Collins</a>, formerly of <a href="/wiki/King_Crimson" title="King Crimson">King Crimson</a>.</p>
