<p><b>Rain</b> (<a href="/wiki/Persian_language" title="Persian language">Persian</a>: <span dir="rtl" lang="fa" xml:lang="fa">رئين</span>‎‎, also <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Romanize" title="Romanize">Romanized</a> as <b>Ra’īn</b>)<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> is a village in <a href="/wiki/Aladagh_Rural_District" title="Aladagh Rural District">Aladagh Rural District</a>, in the <a href="/wiki/Central_District_(Bojnord_County)" title="Central District (Bojnord County)">Central District</a> of <a href="/wiki/Bojnord_County" title="Bojnord County">Bojnord County</a>, <a href="/wiki/North_Khorasan_Province" title="North Khorasan Province">North Khorasan Province</a>, <a href="/wiki/Iran" title="Iran">Iran</a>. At the 2006 census, its population was 917, in 235 families.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
