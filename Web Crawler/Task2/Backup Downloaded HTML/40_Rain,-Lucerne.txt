<p><b>Rain</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Municipalities_of_Switzerland" title="Municipalities of Switzerland">municipality</a> in the district of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Hochdorf_(district)" title="Hochdorf (district)">Hochdorf</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Cantons_of_Switzerland" title="Cantons of Switzerland">canton</a> of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Lucerne_(canton)" title="Lucerne (canton)">Lucerne</a> in <a href="/wiki/Switzerland" title="Switzerland">Switzerland</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Geography">Geography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Rain,_Lucerne&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Geography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Rain has an area of 9.4 km<sup>2</sup> (3.6 sq mi). Of this area, 74.9% is used for agricultural purposes, while 16.8% is forested. The rest of the land, (8.3%) is settled.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-SFSO_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-SFSO-3">[3]</a></sup> In the 1997 land survey<sup class="plainlinks noprint asof-tag update" style="display:none;"><a class="external text" href="//en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Rain,_Lucerne&amp;action=edit">[update]</a></sup>, 16.77% of the total land area was forested. Of the agricultural land, 69.64% is used for farming or pastures, while 5.31% is used for orchards or vine crops. Of the settled areas, 5.63% is covered with buildings, 0.32% is industrial, 0.74% is classed as special developments, 0.11% is parks or greenbelts and 1.49% is transportation infrastructure.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Demographics">Demographics</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Rain,_Lucerne&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Demographics">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Rain has a population (as of 2007<sup class="plainlinks noprint asof-tag update" style="display:none;"><a class="external text" href="//en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Rain,_Lucerne&amp;action=edit">[update]</a></sup>) of 2,214, of which 5.1% are foreign nationals. Over the last 10 years the population has grown at a rate of 30.9%. Most of the population (as of 2000<sup class="plainlinks noprint asof-tag update" style="display:none;"><a class="external text" href="//en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Rain,_Lucerne&amp;action=edit">[update]</a></sup>) speaks German (96.5%), with Albanian being second most common ( 0.7%) and Portuguese being third ( 0.5%).</p>
<p>In the 2007 election the most popular party was the <a href="/wiki/Christian_Democratic_People%27s_Party_of_Switzerland" title="Christian Democratic People's Party of Switzerland">CVP</a> which received 41.6% of the vote. The next three most popular parties were the <a href="/wiki/Free_Democratic_Party_of_Switzerland" title="Free Democratic Party of Switzerland">FDP</a> (26.2%), the <a href="/wiki/Swiss_People%27s_Party" title="Swiss People's Party">SVP</a> (22.6%) and the <a href="/wiki/Social_Democratic_Party_of_Switzerland" title="Social Democratic Party of Switzerland">SPS</a> (3.9%).</p>
<p>The age distribution in Rain is; 652 people or 28.4% of the population is 0–19 years old. 651 people or 28.4% are 20–39 years old, and 761 people or 33.2% are 40–64 years old. The senior population distribution is 175 people or 7.6% are 65–79 years old, 47 or 2% are 80–89 years old and 9 people or 0.4% of the population are 90+ years old.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-LUSTAT_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-LUSTAT-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
<p>In Rain about 78.5% of the population (between age 25-64) have completed either non-mandatory <a href="/wiki/Education_in_Switzerland#Secondary" title="Education in Switzerland">upper secondary education</a> or additional higher education (either university or a <i><a href="/wiki/Fachhochschule" title="Fachhochschule">Fachhochschule</a></i>).</p>
<p>As of 2000<sup class="plainlinks noprint asof-tag update" style="display:none;"><a class="external text" href="//en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Rain,_Lucerne&amp;action=edit">[update]</a></sup> there are 637 households, of which 141 households (or about 22.1%) contain only a single individual. 110 or about 17.3% are large households, with at least five members.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-LUSTAT_4-1"><a href="#cite_note-LUSTAT-4">[4]</a></sup> As of 2000<sup class="plainlinks noprint asof-tag update" style="display:none;"><a class="external text" href="//en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Rain,_Lucerne&amp;action=edit">[update]</a></sup> there were 335 inhabited buildings in the municipality, of which 252 were built only as housing, and 83 were <a href="/wiki/Mixed-use_development" title="Mixed-use development">mixed use</a> buildings. There were 171 single family homes, 44 double family homes, and 37 multi-family homes in the municipality. Most homes were either two (120) or three (89) story structures. There were only 20 single story buildings and 23 four or more story buildings.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-LUSTAT_4-2"><a href="#cite_note-LUSTAT-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
<p>Rain has an unemployment rate of 0.94%. As of 2005<sup class="plainlinks noprint asof-tag update" style="display:none;"><a class="external text" href="//en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Rain,_Lucerne&amp;action=edit">[update]</a></sup>, there were 156 people employed in the <a href="/wiki/Primary_sector_of_the_economy" title="Primary sector of the economy">primary economic sector</a> and about 52 businesses involved in this sector. 157 people are employed in the <a href="/wiki/Secondary_sector_of_the_economy" title="Secondary sector of the economy">secondary sector</a> and there are 23 businesses in this sector. 276 people are employed in the <a href="/wiki/Tertiary_sector_of_the_economy" title="Tertiary sector of the economy">tertiary sector</a>, with 45 businesses in this sector.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-SFSO_3-1"><a href="#cite_note-SFSO-3">[3]</a></sup> As of 2000<sup class="plainlinks noprint asof-tag update" style="display:none;"><a class="external text" href="//en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Rain,_Lucerne&amp;action=edit">[update]</a></sup> 52.2% of the population of the municipality were employed in some capacity. At the same time, females made up 40.9% of the workforce.</p>
<p>In the 2000 census<sup class="plainlinks noprint asof-tag update" style="display:none;"><a class="external text" href="//en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Rain,_Lucerne&amp;action=edit">[update]</a></sup> the religious membership of Rain was; 1,580 (86.3%) were Roman Catholic, and 126 (6.9%) were Protestant, with an additional 1 (0.05%) that were of some other Christian faith. There are 23 individuals (1.26% of the population) who are Muslim. Of the rest; there were 9 (0.49%) individuals who belong to another religion, 57 (3.11%) who do not belong to any organized religion, 35 (1.91%) who did not answer the question.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-LUSTAT_4-3"><a href="#cite_note-LUSTAT-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
