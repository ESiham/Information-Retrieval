<p><b>Rains</b> is an <a href="/wiki/Unincorporated_area" title="Unincorporated area">unincorporated community</a> in <a href="/wiki/Marion_County,_South_Carolina" title="Marion County, South Carolina">Marion County, South Carolina</a>, United States.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-gnis_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-gnis-1">[1]</a></sup> Located along <a href="/wiki/U.S._Route_501_in_South_Carolina" title="U.S. Route 501 in South Carolina">US 501</a>, 7.5 miles (12.1 km) southeast of <a href="/wiki/Marion,_South_Carolina" title="Marion, South Carolina">Marion</a>. Along the <a href="/wiki/CSX_Transportation" title="CSX Transportation">CSX</a> <a href="/wiki/Seaboard_Air_Line_Railroad" title="Seaboard Air Line Railroad">Seaboard Air Line</a>, the area is a predominantly farming community and quarry station.</p>
