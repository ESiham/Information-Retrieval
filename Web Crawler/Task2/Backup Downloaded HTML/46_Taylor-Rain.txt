<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Taylor_Rain&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Rain first entered the adult-film industry in November 2001. In 2003 she signed an exclusive two-year performing contract with Legend Video that would allow her to cut back on the number of scenes she performed in and also provide her with a private chauffeur.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-legend_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-legend-3">[3]</a></sup> Legend released her from her contract less than a month later, and she considered retirement,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-legend2_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-legend2-4">[4]</a></sup> but instead only took a brief hiatus from performing.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
<p>In August 2004 she signed a non-exclusive promotional contract with Astrux Entertainment.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup></p>
<p>In 2005 she made her directorial debut with the 1st Strike Films release <i>Ass Up Face Down</i>, in which she also starred.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup> That same year she signed a contract with Defiance Films and Torrid Entertainment<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-defiance_8-0"><a href="#cite_note-defiance-8">[8]</a></sup> and auctioned a day with her on set on <a href="/wiki/EBay" title="EBay">eBay</a> for $3,900.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ebay_9-0"><a href="#cite_note-ebay-9">[9]</a></sup></p>
<p>Rain was the winner of the "Porn Star Beauty Pageant" on the <a href="/wiki/Howard_Stern" title="Howard Stern">Howard Stern</a> Radio Show in March 2005, beating out fellow porn stars Kami Andrews, Railee, Renee Pornero and <a href="/wiki/Aurora_Snow" title="Aurora Snow">Aurora Snow</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-stern_10-0"><a href="#cite_note-stern-10">[10]</a></sup></p>
<p>Off screen, Rain won control over Scott Fayner's porn industry gossip site Lukeford.com.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-defiance_8-1"><a href="#cite_note-defiance-8">[8]</a></sup></p>
<p>In December 2005 she announced her retirement from acting in pornographic movies,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-retirement_11-0"><a href="#cite_note-retirement-11">[11]</a></sup> expressing a desire to settle down and start a family.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-retirement_11-1"><a href="#cite_note-retirement-11">[11]</a></sup> Despite her retirement, Defiance Films extended her contract for two years.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-12"><a href="#cite_note-12">[12]</a></sup> She launched her website, clubtaylorrain.com, after retiring.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-13"><a href="#cite_note-13">[13]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personal_life">Personal life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Taylor_Rain&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Personal life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In June 2006, she announced via her website that she was pregnant,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-p1_14-0"><a href="#cite_note-p1-14">[14]</a></sup> but <a href="/wiki/Miscarriage" title="Miscarriage">miscarried</a> one month later.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-15"><a href="#cite_note-15">[15]</a></sup></p>
<p>In February 2007, she spent 20 days in jail for possession of <a href="/wiki/Cannabis_(drug)" title="Cannabis (drug)">marijuana</a> and for <a href="/wiki/Driving_under_the_influence" title="Driving under the influence">driving under the influence</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-cannabis_16-0"><a href="#cite_note-cannabis-16">[16]</a></sup> Rain wrote she was given the choice to either spend 20 days in jail or to do <a href="/wiki/Community_service" title="Community service">community service</a> and enter a DUI program, and chose the jail time. In 2008, she declared that she had since stopped using and selling drugs, and now attended meetings of <a href="/wiki/Narcotics_Anonymous" title="Narcotics Anonymous">Narcotics Anonymous</a> and <a href="/wiki/Alcoholics_Anonymous" title="Alcoholics Anonymous">Alcoholics Anonymous</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-17"><a href="#cite_note-17">[17]</a></sup></p>
<p>Rain announced that she was pregnant again in March 2008 and that the ultrasound indicated the child would likely be a girl.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-18"><a href="#cite_note-18">[18]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-19"><a href="#cite_note-19">[19]</a></sup></p>
