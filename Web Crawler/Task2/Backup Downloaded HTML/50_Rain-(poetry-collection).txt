<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p><i><b>Rain</b></i> is a collection of poetry by the Scottish poet <a href="/wiki/Don_Paterson" title="Don Paterson">Don Paterson</a>.It was published in 2009 by <a href="/wiki/Faber_and_Faber" title="Faber and Faber">Faber and Faber</a> and won the Forward poetry prize for best poetry collection of the same year.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
