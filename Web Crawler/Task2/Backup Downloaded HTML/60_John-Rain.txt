<p><b>John Rain</b> is a fictional <a href="/wiki/Character_(arts)" title="Character (arts)">character</a> created by <a href="/wiki/Barry_Eisler" title="Barry Eisler">Barry Eisler</a>. He appears in eight of Eisler's <a href="/wiki/Espionage" title="Espionage">espionage</a> novels as an <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Assassin" title="Assassin">assassin</a> for hire who specializes in making his victims appear to have died of natural causes. Rain is a <a href="/wiki/Vietnam_War" title="Vietnam War">Vietnam War</a> veteran who served with the Special Forces; he was later recruited by the CIA, but by the time he appears in the first of the "John Rain" novels, "Rain Fall", he is working "freelance," offering his assassination services to any client willing to follow his three rules: 1) No women or children, 2) No secondary team working separately on an assassination Rain is handling, 3) Principal actors only (i.e., no hits on family members, etc., in order to "send a message").</p>
<p>Rain's years of training in the Special Forces, CIA, and as an assassin for hire manifest themselves in a deep-seated paranoia, an inability to trust anyone, and the drive to eliminate any loose ends (usually through killing) that may leave him vulnerable to payback or revenge.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biographical_information">Biographical information</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=John_Rain&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biographical information">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>John Rain was born <b>Junichi Fujiwara</b> (<a href="/wiki/Japanese_language" title="Japanese language">Japanese</a>: 藤原純一, <i>Fujiwara Junichi</i>) in 1952 to a <a href="/wiki/Japanese_people" title="Japanese people">Japanese</a> father and an <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">American</a> mother. He is a freelance <a href="/wiki/Assassination" title="Assassination">assassin</a> whose speciality is assassinations that appear to be death by natural causes. He has undergone plastic surgery to hide his Caucasian heritage and has dyed his brown hair. In 1960, Rain's father was killed, so his mother moved them to Dryden, NY where she taught at Cornell.</p>
<p>His passions include <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Single_Malt" title="Single Malt">Single Malt</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Whiskey" title="Whiskey">Whiskies</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jazz" title="Jazz">jazz</a>. He is an accomplished <a href="/wiki/Martial_arts" title="Martial arts">martial artist</a> with years of experience training <a href="/wiki/Judo" title="Judo">Judo</a> at the <a href="/wiki/Kodokan" title="Kodokan">Kodokan</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gracie_Jiu-Jitsu" title="Gracie Jiu-Jitsu">Gracie Jiu-Jitsu</a> in <a href="/wiki/Brazil" title="Brazil">Brazil</a>. During his initial contact with Dox in Brazil, he was also shown to be knowledgeable in <a href="/wiki/Sambo_(martial_art)" title="Sambo (martial art)">Sambo</a>, as well. He is also a <a href="/wiki/Vietnam_veteran" title="Vietnam veteran">Vietnam veteran</a> where he served with the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/US_Army" title="US Army">US Army</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Special_Forces" title="Special Forces">Special Forces</a> and had his first encounter with the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/CIA" title="CIA">CIA</a> that later led to his recruitment. After <a href="/wiki/Vietnam_War" title="Vietnam War">Vietnam</a>, he became a mercenary, helping the Lebanese Christians in Beirut and the rebels in Afghanistan. Afterwards, he worked for the Japanese LDP, unwittingly for Yamaoto (a local powermonger) and Holtzer (a rogue CIA operative, later killed by Rain himself).</p>
<p>Rain is a hand-to-hand combat expert, although he is also effective when armed.</p>
<p>The character is featured in <i><a href="/wiki/Rain_Fall" title="Rain Fall">Rain Fall</a></i> a 2009 major motion picture directed by Max Mannix and starring Kippei Shiina and Gary Oldman.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Family">Family</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=John_Rain&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Family">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Koichiro">Koichiro</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=John_Rain&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Koichiro">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>His 2½ year old son, the result of a night with Midori Kawamura, a jazz pianist, who was the daughter of one of Rain's targets. His mother intends to tell him that his father is dead, once he becomes old enough. They live in New York City and his mother has released a CD called "Another Time."</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Allies">Allies</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=John_Rain&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Allies">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Dox">Dox</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=John_Rain&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Dox">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Freelance Assassin, an American former Marine Sniper whom he met in Reagan-era Afghanistan. Built like a linebacker and sports a goatee (although now he sports no goatee since Delilah told him he had nice facial bones). Dox and Rain have formed a deep friendship, the former having turned down a chance at five million dollars in favor of helping the latter, who was badly wounded. Dox speaks with a Southern twang, and masks a deadly persona with an "aw shucks" attitude. He currently resides in Bali in a home that he built himself. Dox was recently captured and tortured by Hilger and his associates. They had a scheme to get revenge on Rain. He is later rescued by John Rain and his allies. First appearance, John Rain #3.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Delilah">Delilah</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=John_Rain&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Delilah">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>A <a href="/wiki/Mossad" title="Mossad">Mossad</a> operative, smart, sexy, and sophisticated. She was born in Haifa, Israel and speaks fluent Hebrew. She stands about 5'6" and has cobalt blue eyes. Her profession with the Mossad is to seduce potential terrorists and gather information about, to enable their assassinations. Rain got to know her as their paths crossed when he had the task to eliminate an arms dealer in Macau, who was also Delilah's target. Afterwards, they became lovers, bound by a mutual understanding for each other's profession. She is usually based in Paris and operates under the guise of a fashion photographer. Known colleagues in the Mossad include Boaz, Gil, and Naftali.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Haruyoshi">Haruyoshi</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=John_Rain&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Haruyoshi">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>aka "Harry", a former NSA employee. Haruyoshi Fukasawa was born in America and went to college and graduate school there, earning a degree in applied mathematics and cryptology. After hacking into too many government sites, he was forced to work for the NSA. In the first two books, he was based in Tokyo, an expert in all things technical. Rain’s unwitting partner, Harry didn’t know his employer's true profession—and never asked. Harry was the classic “otaku – and, in addition to being a computer guru – was a guy who has saved Rain's life countless times by equipping him with the latest gadgetry. Harry had a "man crush" on Rain. In the second book, he fell in love with a Japanese hostess who showed herself to be an "agent" of Yamaoto criminal organization, hired to track John Rain location. Haruyoshi was "disposed" after letting Yamaoto associates find what they were looking for.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Crazy_Jake">Crazy Jake</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=John_Rain&amp;action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Crazy Jake">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Jim Calhoun, a childhood friend who Rain was forced to kill in Vietnam despite his better judgment. He grudgingly let Rain date his sister, Deidre.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Tomohisa_.22Tom.22_Kanezaki">Tomohisa "Tom" Kanezaki</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=John_Rain&amp;action=edit&amp;section=9" title='Edit section: Tomohisa "Tom" Kanezaki'>edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The CIA Chief of Tokyo Station. An American ethnic Japanese. He had a quid pro quo relationship with Rain. A relative novice, he developed into a seasoned spymaster thanks to associations with Rain and Tatsu.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Ishikura_.22Tatsu.22_Tatsuhiko">Ishikura "Tatsu" Tatsuhiko</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=John_Rain&amp;action=edit&amp;section=10" title='Edit section: Ishikura "Tatsu" Tatsuhiko'>edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The former department head of the Keisatsucho, the Japanese FBI. One-time nemesis and later, close friend/adviser to Rain. Later he is gravely ill and asks Rain to euthanize him, which Rain does reluctantly.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Naomi_Nascimento">Naomi Nascimento</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=John_Rain&amp;action=edit&amp;section=11" title="Edit section: Naomi Nascimento">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>A twenty something Brazilian national with green eyes. She met Rain while working in a Tokyo club, eventually becoming a paramour. Being linked to Rain put her in danger, so she relocated to Brazil and went on to own her own bar. The CIA pressed her for information on Rain's whereabouts once he left Japan.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Enemies">Enemies</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=John_Rain&amp;action=edit&amp;section=12" title="Edit section: Enemies">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Jim_Hilger">Jim Hilger</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=John_Rain&amp;action=edit&amp;section=13" title="Edit section: Jim Hilger">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>An ex-CIA operative, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Non-official_Cover" title="Non-official Cover">Non-official Cover</a>, former financial planner based in Hong Kong. He ran a private spy company that took on missions the CIA could not endorse. Hilger had too much dirt on the right people, making him untouchable. He ran Wilson, Demeere, Garza, and Guthrie. With the help of those men, he tried to kill Rain and Dox, but failed. Hilger is always described as a very dangerous man, since he is an expert with handguns. He shot and killed Gil, a highly lethal Mossad assassin. Gil knew him as <i>Huxton</i> and believed him to be active CIA. He is known to keep extremely calm, even in lethal situations, which makes him a person of authority. He has a sister named Sarah that lives in New York City. He later kidnaps Dox to force Rain into doing his bidding. This lead up to a brutal fight between him and Rain, leaving him dead and Rain wounded. Unlike the other enemies, Hilger does not act because of seeking personal gain but out of a sense of patriotism. He also cares very much about his men, being upset when John and Dox have killed them.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Yamaoto_Toshi">Yamaoto Toshi</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=John_Rain&amp;action=edit&amp;section=14" title="Edit section: Yamaoto Toshi">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Powerful Yakuza Boss and politician. He was an expert in judo and had a personal vendetta against Rain after the latter kept a disc containing sensitive information from him. During his time seeking revenge on Rain he became aware of Midori and Rain's son. He used them hoping to draw Rain out of hiding, this however backfired causing only Yamaoto's death.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="William_Holtzer">William Holtzer</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=John_Rain&amp;action=edit&amp;section=15" title="Edit section: William Holtzer">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Former Tokyo Station Chief and Rain's nemesis in Vietnam. He ruined Rain's career path because the latter refused to collaborate in corruption. Was a "mole" and admitted as such.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Guthrie">Guthrie</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=John_Rain&amp;action=edit&amp;section=16" title="Edit section: Guthrie">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>member of Hilger's task force and was among those who kidnapped Dox. Was a Federal Air Marshall known for his effective combat shooting.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Frank_Garza">Frank Garza</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=John_Rain&amp;action=edit&amp;section=17" title="Edit section: Frank Garza">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Also known as Pancho. Was an ex marine, boxer and 4th dan in Kenpo. He was one of Hilger's men and was known for his bad temper. Was also a sadist, who took pleasure in torturing Dox.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Drano">Drano</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=John_Rain&amp;action=edit&amp;section=18" title="Edit section: Drano">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Unfortunate member of Hilger's task force who was killed by Hilger himself. This was due to his arrogance and sloppiness. This was despite the fact that he was an ex Navy SEAL. The nickname Drano was created by himself rather than comrades, which shows him as incredibly <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Narcissistic" title="Narcissistic">narcissistic</a>.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Demeere">Demeere</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=John_Rain&amp;action=edit&amp;section=19" title="Edit section: Demeere">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Belgian member of Hilgers team. Used to work for the DAS guarding embassies. Was proficient in both Tai Chi and knife fighting. Was also known to carry out successful renditions.</p>
