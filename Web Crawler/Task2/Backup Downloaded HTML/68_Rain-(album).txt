<p><i><b>Rain</b></i> is a 2008 album by <a href="/wiki/Joe_Jackson_(musician)" title="Joe Jackson (musician)">Joe Jackson</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Noble_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-Noble-1">[1]</a></sup> It was released by <a href="/wiki/Rykodisc" title="Rykodisc">Rykodisc</a> on 28 January 2008 in the UK and one day later in the U.S.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Noble_1-2"><a href="#cite_note-Noble-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>Jackson plays piano and sings, and <a href="/wiki/Graham_Maby" title="Graham Maby">Graham Maby</a> on bass and David Houghton on drums are the only other musicians. It was recorded at Planet Roc in <a href="/wiki/Berlin" title="Berlin">Berlin</a>, Germany. A <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Limited_edition" title="Limited edition">limited edition</a> version of the album included a CD and a bonus DVD containing over 40 minutes of material, including concert and behind-the-scenes footage and interviews. Jackson performed a full UK <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Concert_tour" title="Concert tour">tour</a>.</p>
