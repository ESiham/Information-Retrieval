<p><i><b>The Rain</b></i> is a 2004 album by the Persian-Indian hybrid ensemble Ghazal, comprising <a href="/wiki/Kamancheh" title="Kamancheh">kamancheh</a> player <a href="/wiki/Kayhan_Kalhor" title="Kayhan Kalhor">Kayhan Kalhor</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> vocalist and <a href="/wiki/Sitar" title="Sitar">sitar</a> player <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Shujaat_Husain_Khan" title="Shujaat Husain Khan">Shujaat Husain Khan</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> and <a href="/wiki/Tabla" title="Tabla">tabla</a> player <a href="/wiki/Sandeep_Das" title="Sandeep Das">Sandeep Das</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> The 2001 live recording was nominated for a Grammy Award in 2004.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
