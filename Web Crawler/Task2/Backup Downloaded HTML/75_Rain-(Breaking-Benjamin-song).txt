<p>"<b>Rain</b>" is a song by American <a href="/wiki/Rock_music" title="Rock music">rock</a> band <a href="/wiki/Breaking_Benjamin" title="Breaking Benjamin">Breaking Benjamin</a>. It was released in June 2005 as the third single from their second album, <i><a href="/wiki/We_Are_Not_Alone_(Breaking_Benjamin_album)" title="We Are Not Alone (Breaking Benjamin album)">We Are Not Alone</a></i>.</p>
<p>The 2005 single version of Rain is found only on newer pressings of <i>We Are Not Alone</i>. This version is a full-band version of "Rain" (as opposed to the original version in which the only instrument used is an acoustic guitar). The song was performed on <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/AOL_Sessions" title="AOL Sessions">AOL Sessions</a> along with "<a href="/wiki/So_Cold_(song)" title="So Cold (song)">So Cold</a>" and "<a href="/wiki/Sooner_or_Later_(Breaking_Benjamin_song)" title="Sooner or Later (Breaking Benjamin song)">Sooner or Later</a>".<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
