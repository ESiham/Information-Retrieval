<p>"<b>Rain</b>" is a song written and performed by <a href="/wiki/Creed_(band)" title="Creed (band)">Creed</a>. It is their second single from their <a href="/wiki/2009_in_music" title="2009 in music">2009</a> album, <i><a href="/wiki/Full_Circle_(Creed_album)" title="Full Circle (Creed album)">Full Circle</a></i>.</p>
<p>The single was released through <a href="/wiki/MSN_Music" title="MSN Music">MSN Music</a> on September 22, 2009, hit radio airplay September 23, 2009, and was released as a digital download on October 6, 2009 on iTunes and other digital retailers.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (September 2009)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup></p>
<p>The feedback for the song has been positive, and has been competing in rank on sites such as iTunes with "<a href="/wiki/Overcome_(Creed_song)" title="Overcome (Creed song)">Overcome</a>".<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (September 2009)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup> It has reached #91 on the <a href="/wiki/Billboard_Hot_100" title="Billboard Hot 100"><i>Billboard</i> Hot 100</a> and #67 on the Billboard Digital Songs.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (January 2010)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup> It has since re-entered the Hot 100 at #91, matching its previous peak.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (January 2010)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup> "Rain" also made its debut in the ARIA Charts at number 66 in Australia.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
