<p>"<b>Rain</b>" is a song by Australian-New Zealand group <a href="/wiki/Dragon_(band)" title="Dragon (band)">Dragon</a> released in July 1983<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Apter_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-Apter-1">[1]</a></sup> as the first single from Dragon's seventh studio album <i><a href="/wiki/Body_and_the_Beat" title="Body and the Beat">Body and the Beat</a></i> (1984). "Rain" first charted on 15 August 1983, peaked at number 2 and stayed in the charts for 26 weeks.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-NZ_Onscreen_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-NZ_Onscreen-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Nichols2016_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-Nichols2016-3">[3]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-NZ_Herals_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-NZ_Herals-4">[4]</a></sup> The song peaked at #88 on the US <a href="/wiki/Billboard_Hot_100" title="Billboard Hot 100"><i>Billboard</i> Hot 100</a> charts.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Times_Union_Blog_5-0"><a href="#cite_note-Times_Union_Blog-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
