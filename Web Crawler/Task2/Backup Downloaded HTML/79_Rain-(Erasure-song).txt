<p><b>"Rain"</b> is a song by British <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Synthpop" title="Synthpop">synthpop</a> duo <a href="/wiki/Erasure" title="Erasure">Erasure</a>. It is the third single from Erasure's eighth studio album, <i><a href="/wiki/Cowboy_(album)" title="Cowboy (album)">Cowboy</a></i> (1997). <a href="/wiki/Mute_Records" title="Mute Records">Mute Records</a> distributed the single on CD and vinyl in the UK and Europe. Some releases, titled <i>Rain Plus</i>, had so many remixes and live recordings, that the number of the tracks and their combined running-time made <i>Rain Plus</i> ineligible for ranking on the <a href="/wiki/UK_Singles_Chart" title="UK Singles Chart">UK Singles Chart</a>.</p>
