<p>"<b>Rain</b>" is a 1997 song by <a href="/wiki/Guano_Apes" title="Guano Apes">Guano Apes</a>. It was first released on January 1998 as a promotional single, but later had a full release on May 1998 as their second single from their debut album <i><a href="/wiki/Proud_Like_a_God" title="Proud Like a God">Proud Like a God</a></i>. It is one of the songs by the band to have a softer sound, followed by "Don't You Turn Your Back on Me", "Living in a Lie", "Pretty in Scarlet" and "Quietly". The music video shows the band walking on a desert, looking for rain.</p>
