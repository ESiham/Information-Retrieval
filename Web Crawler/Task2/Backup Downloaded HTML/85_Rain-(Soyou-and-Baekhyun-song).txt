<p>"<b>Rain</b>" (<a href="/wiki/Korean_language" title="Korean language">Korean</a>: <span lang="ko" xml:lang="ko">비가와</span>) is a song by South Korean singers <a href="/wiki/Soyou" title="Soyou">Soyou</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Baekhyun" title="Baekhyun">Baekhyun</a>, members of <a href="/wiki/K-pop" title="K-pop">K-pop</a> groups <a href="/wiki/Sistar" title="Sistar">Sistar</a> and <a href="/wiki/Exo_(band)" title="Exo (band)">EXO</a> respectively. It was released on February 14, 2017 by <a href="/wiki/Starship_Entertainment" title="Starship Entertainment">Starship Entertainment</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Background_and_release">Background and release</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Rain_(Soyou_and_Baekhyun_song)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Background and release">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>On February 6, 2017, Soyou and Baekhyun were announced by the former's agency <a href="/wiki/Starship_Entertainment" title="Starship Entertainment">Starship Entertainment</a> to be collaborating on a duet titled "Rain".<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> The song reflects the melancholic feelings of nostalgia that accompanies the rain after a relationship comes to an end.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-:0_5-0"><a href="#cite_note-:0-5">[5]</a></sup> On February 9, a teaser video of the two singers recording the song was released.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> On February 14, the song was released digitally accompanied by its music video. On February 15, Starship Entertainment released a behind-the-scenes video of the song's recording session.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Music_video">Music video</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Rain_(Soyou_and_Baekhyun_song)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Music video">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The music video features a high school student reminiscing on a rainy day by herself while listening to music.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-:0_5-1"><a href="#cite_note-:0-5">[5]</a></sup> It hit 1 million views in less than a day from its release. On February 15, Starship Entertainment released a special clip of the music video starring Soyou.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Reception">Reception</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Rain_(Soyou_and_Baekhyun_song)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Reception">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Upon release, "Rain" quickly reached the top on every South Korean online music charts, an achievement known as "all-kill".<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup> The song debuted at number two on the South Korean <a href="/wiki/Gaon_Digital_Chart" title="Gaon Digital Chart">Gaon Digital Chart</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-gaonweekly_9-0"><a href="#cite_note-gaonweekly-9">[9]</a></sup> The song placed first on <a href="/wiki/QQ_Music" title="QQ Music">QQ Music</a>'s K-pop chart as well as on <a href="/wiki/YinYueTai" title="YinYueTai">YinYueTai</a>'s weekly music video chart.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup></p>
