<p>"<b>Rain</b>" is a single released by the British <a href="/wiki/Rock_music" title="Rock music">rock</a> band <a href="/wiki/Status_Quo_(band)" title="Status Quo (band)">Status Quo</a> in 1976. It was written by <a href="/wiki/Rick_Parfitt" title="Rick Parfitt">Rick Parfitt</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> included on the album <i><a href="/wiki/Blue_for_You" title="Blue for You">Blue for You</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<p>The track "Rain" was originally intended to be included on the album <i><a href="/wiki/On_the_Level" title="On the Level">On the Level</a></i>, but at the time of the recording sessions Parfitt had not completed the song and so it was held over to the band's next album release.</p>
<p>The song was reprised, in 2014, for the band's thirty-first studio album <i><a href="/wiki/Aquostic_(Stripped_Bare)" title="Aquostic (Stripped Bare)">Aquostic (Stripped Bare)</a></i>. It was featured in the ninety-minute launch performance of the album at London's <i><a href="/wiki/Roundhouse_(venue)" title="Roundhouse (venue)">Roundhouse</a></i> on 22 October, the concert being recorded and broadcast live by <a href="/wiki/BBC_Radio_2" title="BBC Radio 2">BBC Radio 2</a> as part of their <i>In Concert</i> series.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
