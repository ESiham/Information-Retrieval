<p>"<b>Rain</b>" is the seventeenth single by Japanese pop singer-songwriter <a href="/wiki/Yui_(singer)" title="Yui (singer)">Yui</a>. It was released on November 24, 2010. The title song is a tie-in for the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Fuji_TV" title="Fuji TV">Fuji TV</a> drama series <i>Perfect Report</i>. The single reached number 2 on the <a href="/wiki/Oricon" title="Oricon">Oricon</a> chart.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Music_video_and_lyrics">Music video and lyrics</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Rain_(Yui_song)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Music video and lyrics">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The music video for "Rain" alternates between cuts of Yui and her band playing in the rain and Yui herself running through a rainy street towards a Christmas gift shop. Around the middle of the music video, a happier past is shown through flashbacks of Yui smiling and receiving earrings on a sunny day, along with the lines: "They try to bring back memories / Which is already lost / No matter what I do, I can't get rid of the past."<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[note 1]</a></sup> The video eventually ends with an image of hope for the future, with the lyrics: "When someday this rain turns to snow / And slowly consumes this sadness / I'll be greeted by new hope.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ex02_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-ex02-3">[note 2]</a></sup></p>
